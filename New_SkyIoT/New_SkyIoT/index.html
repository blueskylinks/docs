Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: What is the SkyIoT Water Tank Overflow Alarm System?

The SkyIoT Water Tank Overflow Alarm System is a smart device designed to prevent water wastage by alerting users when their tank is full. It operates using advanced sensors and notifies users via alarms with loud beep sound.

Q2: How does the automated controller work?

The automated controller regulates the water flow by turning the motor on and off based on the water level. It ensures optimal water usage without manual intervention.

Q3: What types of water tanks are compatible with SkyIoT devices?

SkyIoT devices are compatible with various types of water tanks, including plastic, cement, and underground tanks. The sensors work efficiently with both residential and commercial setups.

Q4: How do I install the SkyIoT Water Tank Overflow Alarm System?

Installation is simple. Mount the sensor in the water tank at the desired level, connect it to the control unit, and configure the settings via the mobile app (if applicable). A detailed installation guide is provided with the device.

Q5: Does the system require professional installation?

No, it can be installed easily by following the provided manual. However, for advanced configurations or automated controllers, professional installation is recommended.

Q6: Does the system work on both AC and DC power?

Yes, the system supports both AC and DC power sources, making it versatile for different locations.
